How to inspect this lens

A Kakobuy QC photo set is evidence, not decoration. For pants & shorts qc, start by checking whether the photo set shows the areas that usually reveal problems. If the most important detail is missing, the correct decision is not approval; it is a retake request or a hold.

Compare the warehouse photos against product-page references and any trusted community examples you used during discovery. The goal is not to find microscopic differences in every image. The goal is to confirm that the actual item matches the reason you wanted it, and that no obvious flaw will become expensive after international shipping.

Pass signals

  • Required angles are clear, uncropped, and bright enough to inspect.
  • Labels, tags, prints, stitching, or hardware match the category expectation.
  • Measurements or size evidence support the size you ordered.
  • Pair, set, or multi-part items are complete and consistent.
  • Parcel weight and packaging do not erase the value of the find.

Retake signals

Request a retake when the key detail is blurry, covered, too far away, hidden by packaging, or missing entirely. A retake should be specific: ask for the label, sole, print, zipper, measurement, or damaged area you need to inspect.
