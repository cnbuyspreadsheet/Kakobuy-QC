About this Kakobuy QC desk

This site focuses on the photo-review moment after an item reaches the warehouse. It helps buyers read QC evidence, request useful retakes, and avoid rushing weak items into international parcels.

It is independent, informational, and does not process purchases, payments, returns, or shipping support.

This site is an independent guide and is not the official Kakobuy website. Product listings, prices, QC options, agent availability, and shipping details may change. Always verify details on the linked product page and selected shopping agent before ordering.
